Welcome to Our Temple

Experience Divine Blessings

Varada Vinayak Ganesh Mandir, located at the heart of Belgaum at RTO Circle, is a sacred abode of Lord Ganesha where devotees find peace, prosperity, and spiritual fulfillment.

Our temple is renowned for its serene atmosphere and the powerful presence of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. Established over 50 years ago, the temple has been serving the spiritual needs of thousands of devotees.

About Our Temple

History & Significance

Varada Vinayak Ganesh Mandir was established in 1970 [&reestablished in 2025] by a group of devoted followers of Lord Ganesha. The temple's main deity is a beautiful 3-foot tall idol of Lord Ganesha in the Varada Vinayak form, blessing devotees with his right hand.

The temple gained prominence when several devotees reported their wishes being fulfilled after sincere prayers. Since then, the temple has become a spiritual hub in Belgaum.

The idol is believed to be self-manifested (Swayambhu)
